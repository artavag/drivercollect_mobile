How does it works

Step 1: Create a request

Type your desired "from" and "to" directions as well as date and time values in form and hit the "save" button.

At this point you can also make your request more customized by clicking on "Refine search button".

Step 2: We begin searching for you

At this point you don't have to do anything.

You get displayed all found matches.

If no match was fount, we keep searching for you. As soon as Drivercollect finds unique matches for you, an email is sent to you.

Step 3: Match found

Drivercollect records the agreement and sends the details.

Step 4: You agree to the rides that suit you

  • Drivercollect search 24 x 7 for you.
  • As soon as suitable matches are spotted, Drivercollect inform you.
  • Drivercollect ranks the top matches for you.
  • Drivercollect lets you decide what matches you want to select and which you want to reject.
